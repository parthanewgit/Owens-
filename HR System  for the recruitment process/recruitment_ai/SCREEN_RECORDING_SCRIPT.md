# Recruitment AI System - Screen Recording Script (1 Minute)

## Speech for Screen Recording Demonstration

---

**[INTRO - 0-10 seconds]**

"Welcome to the HR Recruitment AI System – an intelligent platform that transforms the hiring process using artificial intelligence. This application combines advanced NLP and machine learning to streamline candidate evaluation and assessment."

**[KEY FEATURES - 10-35 seconds]**

"The system features three core capabilities: First, our Resume Intelligence Agent analyzes candidate qualifications automatically. Second, the AI-powered Question Generator creates customized, role-specific interview questions. Third, the Answer Evaluation Agent assesses candidate responses with detailed scoring and feedback."

**[DEMONSTRATION - 35-50 seconds]**

"Users access the system through our intuitive Streamlit interface. Simply upload resumes, generate tailored questions, and collect candidate answers. The AI then evaluates responses instantly, providing scores and detailed analysis to support hiring decisions."

**[CLOSING - 50-60 seconds]**

"Powered by GPT-4 and advanced embeddings, this system reduces hiring time while improving candidate quality assessment. The technology learns from evaluations, continuously improving its effectiveness. Transform your recruitment process today with Recruitment AI."

---

## Timing Breakdown:
- **Introduction:** 10 seconds
- **Features:** 25 seconds  
- **Demonstration:** 15 seconds
- **Closing:** 10 seconds
- **Total:** ~60 seconds

## Tips for Recording:
1. Speak clearly at a moderate pace (130-140 words per minute)
2. Pause briefly between sections for transitions
3. Sync screen changes with relevant speech segments
4. Show UI elements as you describe each feature
